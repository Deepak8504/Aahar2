Donations that help Change lives

Aahar is a volunteer based organization that works to get surplus food from restaurants and community to serve less fortunate people.

Process of AAHAR

1

Food is saved

Annually 1.5 tonne food is wasted. 0.02 tonne of waste food is utilised for the poor.

2

Drive is conducted

400 drives are conducted every year. 512 people have volunteered.

3

Food is distributed

15000+ hungry people are fed. 500000+ people are remaining.
